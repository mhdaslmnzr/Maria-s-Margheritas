Where Every Slice Feels Like Home

Welcome to Maria's Margheritas, where every slice feels like home. Based in the beautiful coastal region of Thirumullavaram, Kollam, our home bakery offers an intimate and authentic pizza experience. Founded by Deepthi Maria John, we specialize in crafting mouth-watering Margherita pizzas and a variety of other delicious options right from our home kitchen. At Maria's Margheritas, we use only the freshest ingredients and traditional recipes to ensure every pizza is both scrumptious and nourishing. Our commitment to quality and our passion for pizza-making shine through in every bite, making each pizza a true labor of love. Join us at Maria's Margheritas for a pizza experience that feels like it was made just for you. Whether you're a local resident or a visitor, we invite you to savor the best home-baked pizzas Kollam has to offer.
